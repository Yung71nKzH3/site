Introduction

While artificial intelligence (AI) often dominates public discourse, the concurrent rise of sophisticated robotics is equally transformative. These two fields are intricately intertwined, driving rapid advancements in technology and profoundly impacting human society. From industrial automation to assistive technologies, robotics is rapidly reshaping our world, raising critical questions about the evolving relationship between humans and machines.

This literature review investigates how public opinion on human-robot interactions will likely change in the future. To address this, the review will delve into key factors such as the safety and ethical considerations surrounding robotics, the influence of technological advancements, and the role of media portrayals in shaping public perceptions.

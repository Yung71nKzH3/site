Introduction

Robotics is almost an underlooked part when considered in the media compared to AI. Truthfully, they go hand in hand when shaping the future of the technology industry, the world, and the fundamentals of the human experience. 1

Starting from AI Chatbots powerful enough to snap the Turing Test in half with now a market for service-type robots, The public are now having to consider these futuristic advancements bringing up the question of how long until robots are entirely assisting or even replacing us. And with only dreamt success already being apparent with Neuralink acting as the cherry on top, How long until they take over?

With robotics already prophesied to become the future for us all and with private organizations making it happen increasingly our daily lives have already been decided. This new coming age or rather an evolution assisted by the powerhouse of AI raises the overarching question of whether it's even the right direction or what truly is and will be the relationship between human-robot interaction in the future.

This literature review investigates the dynamic nature of public opinion on human-robot interaction, exploring how these perceptions are likely to shift in the future. The review will examine key factors influencing this evolution, including:

  • Technological Advancements: Analyzing the impact of cutting-edge developments in robotics, such as advancements in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics, as exemplified by the works of Boston Dynamics, Nvidia, Tesla and Google (just to name a few) on public perceptions.

  • Public Opinion on Specific Applications: Investigating public attitudes towards robots in various domains, including healthcare, and the workplace, exploring concerns about job displacement and the potential for robots to augment or replace human capabilities.

  • Factors Influencing Public Opinion: Examining the multifaceted influences on public opinion, including media portrayals, cultural values, ethical considerations, and the role of trust and safety in the human-robot relationship.

  • Ethical Considerations and Public Debate: Analyzing the ethical dilemmas surrounding robotics, including issues of privacy, autonomy, bias, and the responsible development and deployment of robotic systems.

  • Future Trends and Predictions: Exploring potential future scenarios for human-robot interaction, including the concept of "cybernathrope" – the convergence of human and machine – and its implications for society.

By examining existing research and analyzing current trends, this review aims to provide insights into the potential trajectories of public opinion and their implications for the future of human-robot coexistence.
